Caesar Salad

A Caesar salad is a crisp and creamy delight, perfectly balancing fresh greens with a rich, tangy dressing. The heart of this dish lies in its smooth, garlicky dressing, made with anchovies, Parmesan cheese, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, and a touch of Worcestershire sauce, all blended into a velvety texture. Tossed with crunchy romaine lettuce, it's a perfect blend of bold and refreshing flavors.

Topped with crunchy croutons and extra Parmesan, the salad has a satisfying combination of textures. The savory umami of the anchovies and the sharp, nutty Parmesan provide depth, while the lemony dressing adds a refreshing zing. This timeless salad is an irresistible side or a light main course, full of flavor and indulgence.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing

Steps

  1. Toss the lettuce: In a large bowl, add chopped romaine lettuce
  2. Add dressing: Pour the dressing over the lettuce and toss to coat evenly
  3. Top the salad: Sprinkle with croutons and grated Parmesan cheese
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
